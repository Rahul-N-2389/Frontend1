WELCOME TO MY WORLD

Virat kohli

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of the modern era. Known for his fierce determination, aggressive style, and unmatched consistency, Kohli has redefined batting standards across all formats of the game.
                He made his international debut in 2008 and quickly rose through the ranks, becoming the backbone of India's batting lineup. As captain, he led India to historic victories, including a Test series win in Australia and consistent dominance in ODIs and T20s

Rohit sharma

Rohit Sharma, born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, is one of India's most accomplished cricketers and a masterful tactician. Fondly known as the Hitman for his effortless six-hitting ability, Rohit began his international career in 2007 and rose to prominence after being promoted to open the batting in ODIs.
          His elegant stroke play and calm demeanor have earned him accolades across all formats. Rohit holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs — a staggering 264 runs — and is the only player to score three double centuries in the format. As captain, he led India to victory in the 2024 T20 World Cup and the 2025 Champions Trophy.

MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni , born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand, is one of the most iconic figures in Indian cricket history. Known affectionately as Captain Cool and Thala, Dhoni's journey from a small-town ticket collector to a global cricketing superstar is nothing short of cinematic. He made his international debut in 2004 and quickly became known for his fearless batting and lightning-fast wicketkeeping.
As captain, Dhoni led India to three major ICC trophies: the 2007 T20 World Cup, the 2011 ODI World Cup, and the 2013 Champions Trophy — making him the only captain to achieve this feat.

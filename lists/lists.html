Task 7

  • Ingredients
    1. - Basmati rice – 1 cup
    2. - Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato) – 1 cup (chopped)
    3. - Onion – 1 (thinly sliced)
    4. - Green chili – 1 (slit)
    5. - Ginger-garlic paste – 1 tsp
    6. - Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom) – 1 each
    7. - Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
    8. - Salt – to taste
    9. - Water – 2 cups
    10. - Water – 2 cups
    11. - Fresh coriander – for garnish
  • Steps
    1. - Rinse the rice thoroughly and soak it for 20 minutes. Drain before cooking.
    2. - Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and whole spices; sauté until aromatic.
    3. - Add sliced onions and green chili. Cook until onions turn golden.
    4. - Stir in ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
    5. - Add chopped vegetables and cook for 3–4 minutes.
    6. - Add soaked rice and gently mix with the vegetables.
    7. - Pour in 2 cups of water and add salt. Bring to a boil.
    8. - Cover and simmer on low heat for 15–20 minutes until rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
    10. - Fluff gently with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander.
